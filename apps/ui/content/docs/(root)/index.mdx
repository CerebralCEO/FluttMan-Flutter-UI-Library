**FlutMan** is a collection of beautifully designed, accessible, and composable Flutter UI components. Inspired by **shadcn/ui**, it's designed for you to copy, paste, and own—no package dependencies, no abstraction layers.

We believe you should own your UI code. That's why FlutMan components are distributed as standalone Dart files that you download directly into your project using our CLI. Full source code, full control.

## How It Works

Our approach is simple: **you own your code**. We're inspired by the copy-paste ethos of **shadcn/ui**, adapted for the Flutter ecosystem.

Instead of adding a dependency to your `pubspec.yaml`, you get the source code directly:

- **No package dependencies:** Components live in your `lib/ui/components/` folder.
- **Full control:** Edit any component to match your exact needs.
- **Zero abstraction:** See exactly how everything works under the hood.
- **AI-friendly:** Clean, readable code that language models can understand and modify.

## Built for Flutter, Designed for Production

FlutMan is built specifically for **Flutter** applications. Our components follow Flutter best practices and Material Design principles while remaining fully customizable:

## Components, Particles and Atoms

At the core of FlutMan are the **UI Components**—self-contained, reusable widgets that handle their own state and styling. These are the foundation: buttons, inputs, cards, dialogs, and more.

On top of these, we introduce **Particles**. Particles are **pre-assembled patterns**—like authentication forms, data tables, or date pickers—that combine multiple components into ready-to-use solutions. They're a bit more opinionated in design, but still easy to customize, extend, or break apart when needed.

Finally, we go a step further with **Atoms**. Atoms are **API-enhanced Particles**: components that don't just manage UI state locally, but also integrate with external data and services. Think of them as smart components that connect UI to APIs, providing out-of-the-box behaviors for common patterns (authentication flows, scheduling, etc.).

Together, components, particles, and atoms give you a full spectrum:

- **Components** → the foundation
- **Particles** → the patterns
- **Atoms** → the integrations

This layered approach lets you choose the right level of abstraction for your project.

## Built for Humans and AI

We're building an infrastructure for both humans and AI. Our components are written to be clear, readable, and predictable, so that language models can understand, reason about, and modify them.

Every component page includes a **"Copy Prompt"** button that generates an AI-ready instruction for adding that component to your project.
